About Us

We are the Golden Jackets from USA — a select group of professionals recognized by Amazon Web Services with the highest distinction in the AWS community worldwide. The Golden Jacket is awarded to those who have earned all active AWS certifications and demonstrated exceptional contribution through technical leadership, content creation, community building, and innovation. Our mission is to share knowledge, inspire the next generation of cloud leaders, and strengthen USA's presence on the global AWS stage.

This is an independent community, not officially affiliated with Amazon Web Services.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Golden Jacket?

The Golden Jacket is the highest recognition in the AWS certification ecosystem — awarded to professionals who earn all 12 active AWS certifications. It was created by Geries AbouAyash, Managing Director at AWS.

How many certifications do I need?

All 12 active AWS certifications must be current and valid: 2 Foundational, 5 Associate, 2 Professional, and 3 Specialty level.

How long does it take?

It varies — some achieve it in 1-2 years of focused study, others take longer. The key is consistency and dedication.

How rare is it?

Extremely rare. Less than 0.01% of AWS certified professionals worldwide hold all active certifications. In USA, there are 10+ known Golden Jacket holders.
